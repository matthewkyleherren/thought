HarkerDesign

Launch project
  • Client: Harker Design
  • Type: Website
  • Deliverables: Development, UI, UX, Concept

Harker Design is a nationally renowned, full-service interior design firm. With locations in Utah, Idaho, and Wyoming, they create a balance between a client's aesthetic, lifestyle, and Harker’s expertise.

A picture is worth a thousand words, and when your pictures are as evocative and captivating as the ones Harker Design displays, they are worth their own gallery.

As a leader in the industry, it was necessary for us that Harker Design had a sophisticated website to showcase their designs and better reach their clients adequately. Part of that sophisticated design is presenting a dedicated gallery for their work.

The what is excellent but the where is just as important, so we made getting to the shop easy, and clear. We created custom maps that take you to the location but never take you out of the feeling of the site.

Finding the shop but staying in the feeling of the website, the design aesthetic of the client’s work was important. Many sites add maps as an afterthought, but Harker Design is meticulous about the look, so that is reflected even on the maps page.

Most people think about their home design when they’re on the go; having a mobile experience that was just as exciting, just as compelling, was imperative.

Shopping happens when it happens; that goes for interior design as well. Harker Design needed a mobile experience that smartphone users could key into and be excited about. It needed to be smooth, engaging, and as resplendent as their desktop site, catch the eye, pull them in and convert.
