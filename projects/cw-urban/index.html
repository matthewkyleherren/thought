CWUrban

Launch project
  • Client: CW Urban
  • Type: Website
  • Deliverables: Development, UI, UX, Concept

CW Urban knows the perfect house isn’t just about floor plans, fixtures, or the number of bedrooms and bathrooms. They know the ideal home is also about finding the ideal place. It only made sense that we gave them the perfect place online with a website as unique, stylish, and modern as they are.

Modern & Stylish

Smooth UI and engaging UX are what we provided to elicit a positive emotional response to C.W. Urban’s website. Our development and concept were crafted to match their modern feeling, capture their ease in the community, and highlight their keen eye for stylish spaces that instantly feel like the place to be.

Less Digital More IRL

We made sure the site elements worked together to get the client off the site and into the homes. Pretty pictures are fine for walls, but stunning images, good form, and superior functionality expertly combined take the client off the page and in the front door.

Uniquely Unique

The word unique is ephemeral & becomes a trap in lesser- skilled hands. We were able to see the “what” and “why” of C.W. Urban’s uniqueness, build on it, focus in, and finally give it a corporeal representation that clients could hold and eventually move into.
